Kyoto Studio Park



his new tourist attraction combines the Kyoto studio of Toei Co., Ltd., with a theme park for movie fans. Some of the film sets that are open to the public have full-scale replicas of city streets in the Edo period (1603-1867) and samurai houses. You might see a crew filming a feature movie or a period drama for TV. Another attraction is a 3D adventure in old Japan. At the Shashin-kan, you can dress up as a samurai warrior or ninja spy and have your picture taken. The theme park also goes by the nickname, "Japan Hollywood."
