Map

Kyoto is situated on a plain with mountains nearby in the west, north and east. The streets were laid out centuries ago like a checkerboard. In Kyoto, you can still enjoy the atmosphere of old Japan.


Zoom

Three recommended routes:


  • Eastern Kyoto: Sanjusangendo (3) ~ Kiyomizu-dera Temple (5) ~ Yasaka Pagoda (6) ~ Yasaka Shrine (7) ~ Gion ~ Chion-in Temple (8) ~ Heian Shrine (9) ~ Nanzen-ji Temple (10) ~ Ginkaku-ji Temple (Temple of the Silver Pavilion) (13)
  • Central Kyoto: Kyoto Tower (16) ~ Higashi Hongan-ji Temple (17) … Nijo Castle (22) ~ Kyoto Imperial Palace (23) ~ Nishijin district ~ Kitano Tenman-gu Shrine (25)
